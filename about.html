I help ecommerce and D2C brands turn their Shopify stores into sales-driven, trustworthy experiences — not just good-looking websites.

How do I achieve this?

I design Ecommerce store experiences with a strong focus on both UX (user experience) and UI (user interface) — because how a store works is just as important as how it looks.

UX design relates to how well the website works, what the user experiences and how they feel when using it, and UI design focuses on how that software product looks. Both are important considerations and play a vital role when designing Shopify websites and apps.

Alongside design, I implement on-page SEO best practices, Google Analytics integration, and basic email automation so your store is not only discoverable on search engines, but also set up to track user behaviour and convert visitors more effectively. Every store is built with long-term visibility, usability, and growth in mind.

Other things I enjoy...

Apart from design, I'm drawn to things that slow me down and make me more present — cooking, photography, movement, and time in nature. These moments help me stay curious and observant, and they influence how I design thoughtfully.
